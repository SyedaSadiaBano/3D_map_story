Sindh Coastal Catastrophe
Karachi's 16 million residents face existential threat from sea level rise. By 2050, IPCC SSP5-8.5 projects 65cm inundation, submerging 72% of buildings and displacing 11.5M people. Informal katcha settlements are most vulnerable.
